About Gaspar

I am a business graduate and design student at Pontifical Catholic University of Chile. Studying both disciplines has allowed me to complement creativity with a business vision, both necessary in order to thrive in the design world. In my academic years I have developed skills related to new forms of design such as speculative design, bio design, circular economy and service design. I have also gained special interest in social design where the mix of my two areas of expertise can lead to real impact.
